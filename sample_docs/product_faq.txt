Product FAQ

What platforms are supported?
The app runs on iOS and Android. A web version is planned for next quarter.

How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Privacy > Export data. You will receive a download link by email within 24 hours. The export includes your profile, activity history, and generated content in JSON format.

Is my data encrypted?
Yes. Data in transit uses TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. We do not store payment card numbers; payments are handled by our certified payment provider.

Can I use the API?
API access is available on Pro and Team plans. Documentation and API keys are in the developer section of your account dashboard. Rate limits apply per plan tier.
